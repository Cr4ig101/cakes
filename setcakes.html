Set Cakes

Set cakes are pre designed cakes that have been beautifully design by Victoria. These cakes are every bit as delicious and beautiful as custom cakes. Their lower price in comparison to custom cakes is due to a more streamlined process as there is no additional time needed to design, research or source additional elements for the cake.
You can choose to personalise the cake with a name and age and on some designs choose the colour.
These signature tall cakes are made up of 4 layers of sponge layered with buttercream.
Set cakes come in a variety of sizes see below for the portion guide.

Portion guide
4 inch - 8 portions
5 inch - 16 portions
6 inch - 24 portions
7 inch - 32 portions
8 inch - 40 portions
