Custom Cakes

Cakes by Victoria Marie specialise in custom made cakes perfect for any celebration ranging from baby showers to anniversary celebrations.
Our cakes are available in a range of sizes made up of 4 layers of delicious sponge, layered with a light buttercream. These luxurious custom cakes are design by Victoria. We will liase with you to ensure your cake is perfect for your occaision using a variety of techniques to create a cake that is one of a kind. Additional decorations include cake toppers, fresh flowers, chocolates and many more.

Number Cakes

Number cakes make a perfect party centerpiece to celebrate those milestone birthdays.
Each number cake is made up of 2 layers of fluffy sponge layered and decorated with beautifully piped buttercream which can be customised to suite your colour scheme or theme. Additional decorations include a name topper, flowers, chocolates, and many more.

Number cakes can be be created in a small or large size depending on your party size.

Minimum of 10 portions starting from £65
Decorations are an additional cost
