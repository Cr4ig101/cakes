Custom Cakes

Cakes by Victoria Marie specialise in custom made cakes perfect for any celebration ranging from baby showers to anniversary celebrations.
These cakes are available in a range of sizes made up of 4 layers of delicious sponge, layered with a light buttercream. These luxurious custom cakes are design by Victoria to your specification. We will liase with you and provide a sketch, along with a mood board to ensure your cake is perfect for your occasion and create a cake that is one of a kind.

6 inch - 24 portions

Tiered cakes are also available.

Custom cakes are entirely made to your reqirements, there is a minimum order of £100.


Number Cakes

Number cakes make a perfect party centerpiece to celebrate those milestone birthdays.
Each number cake is made up of 2 layers of fluffy sponge layered and decorated with beautifully piped buttercream which can be customised to suite your colour scheme or theme. Additional decorations include a name topper, flowers, chocolates, and many more.

Number cakes can be be created in a small or large size depending on your party size.

A single number starts from £70
Decorations are an additional cost

Lambeth Cakes

Lambeth cakes are a vintage style cake beautifully piped and decorated.
They are perfect for any occasion and are available in a variety of sizes. Once you have have chosen your colour scheme, decide if you would like simple or elaborate piping and leave the rest to us.

Lambeth cakes are available in round or heart shape.

6 inch lambeth cake servers 12 starts from £70
8 inch lambeth cake servers 22 starts from £90
*Larger sizes available on request

Mini Cakes

Mini cakes are great for a small gathering and make the perfect gift. Each mini cake is made up of 2 deliciously light 5 inch layers of sponge, sandwiched with buttercream and decorated in a colour scheme of your choice. These cakes are perfect for 4-6 portions. All mini cakes include a personalised message on top in fondant or card.

A 5 inch mini cake starts from £40.
